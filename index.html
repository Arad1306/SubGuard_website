FAQ

Do I need payments to use SubGuard?

No. You can run manual onboarding and expiries. Payments are optional and can be enabled later.

How are members added?

Share a secure one‑time link. SubGuard verifies the join and tracks their access window.

Can multiple admins manage the same group?

Yes. Invite admins with role‑based permissions so you can split responsibilities safely.

What analytics do I get?

Active members, churn, renewals, and group activity trends in a simple dashboard.
